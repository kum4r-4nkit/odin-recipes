Besan Ladoo

Besan Ladoo is a popular Indian sweet dish made using gluten free gram flour, sugar and ghee. This is author's mom foolproof recipe followed for years. Also one of my favorite sweets

Ingredients

  • 270 grams – 1 Cup – Besan / Gram Flour
  • 160 gms – 3/4 Cup Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 teaspoon green Cardamom seeds (Elaichi)
  • 250 gms – 1 Cup – Powdered Sugar
  • 2 tablespoon Cashews chopped (Optional)

Instructions

  1. In a heavy bottom pan add in 1/2 Cup of the ghee. Remaining ghee should be added only if required.
  2. 160 gms – 3/4 Cup Ghee
  3. Add in the Besan/Gram Flour and dry roast on slow until it releases a nice aroma.
  4. 270 grams – 1 Cup – Besan / Gram Flour
  5. Add more ghee if you feel the besan is getting too dry and is yet not roasted.
  6. The roasting process takes time around 45 minutes to 1 hour. Be patient.
  7. Once fragrant and you see ghee starts releasing the edges you are done.
  8. Switch off and let this cool completely.
  9. Add the required sugar. If you have sugar granules, crush them in a blender to get a fine powder.
  10. 250 gms – 1 Cup – Powdered Sugar
  11. Remove the seeds from cardamom and add it as is. The ladoo taste better with the seeds not crushed completely.
  12. 1 teaspoon green Cardamom seeds
  13. If adding cashews add it now.
  14. 2 tablespoon Cashews chopped
  15. Take a dollop of the mixtue and form round balls. Size can be as per your preference.
  16. Store it in an air tight container. They stay good for more than a month.
