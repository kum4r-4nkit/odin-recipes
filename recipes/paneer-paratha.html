Paneer Paratha

The Paneer paratha recipe is a delicious and popular Indian whole wheat flatbread stuffed with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), spices, and herbs. Serve hot with a raita or curry of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 100 gms of Paneer crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon of Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • 2 tablespoon fresh coriander chopped
  • Finely chopped green chillies (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilly Powder (optional)
  • Whole Wheat dough for 6-7 Paratha’s
  • Salt
  • Ghee for shallow frying paratha’s

Instructions

  1. Crumble the paneer finely so that you do not have any big lumps.
  2. In a large bowl, add in the paneer , Jeera, coriander , Green Chillies, Red Chilly and Salt.
  3. Crush the paneer and mix the ingredients uniformly.
  4. Be sure that you do not have any paneer lumps.
  5. Take a lemon size ball of dough and roll it around 1 inch diameter.
  6. Apply a little Ghee in the centre. (just dip your finger and apply it).
  7. Add around 1 tablespoon of paneer mixture in it.
  8. Now seal the dough from all sides and for a ball.
  9. Dip it into a the dry whole wheat flour and lightly pat it between your palms so that the paneer mixtures gets evened out.
  10. Start rolling the paratha to the desired size (4-5 inch diameter). Do not turn in between just keep on rolling the paratha. If required apply dry whole wheat flour in between to roll the paratha’s.
  11. Once rolled, put it on a hot greased tawa or a non-stick pan, on slow flame.
  12. Flip it after 40-50 seconds.
  13. Let it cook again on a slow flame for 1-2 mins or until brown spots start appearing all over.
  14. Then apply a little Ghee and cook for a min. Turn apply Ghee and again cook.
  15. Continue cooking on both sides until golden spots appear on both the sides and the paratha is evenly cooked.
